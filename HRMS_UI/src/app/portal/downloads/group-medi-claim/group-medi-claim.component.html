Checklist Documents:

  • Hospital Mail Bill with breakup
  • Discharge Summary with Stamp and Sign
  • Payment Receipt’s
  • Investigation bill’s and reports (Ultrasound report for Maternity claims)
  • Medicine bills with prescription
  • Cancelled cheque with name
  • IOL Implant Sticker & Invoice (For Stent implants cases only)
  • Employee id card copy
  • Aadhar Card copy of patient.

All the documents to be submitted within 30 days from the date of discharge
